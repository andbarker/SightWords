Quick Parent Guide

What are Sight Words?

Sight words are common words that young readers should recognize instantly without sounding out. Mastering these words helps children read more fluently and confidently.

Using Flashcards

  • Click on cards to flip between the word and example sentence
  • Use the sound button to hear the word pronounced
  • Practice for 5-10 minutes daily for best results

Quiz Tips

  • Select the correct word to complete each sentence
  • Encourage your child to read the full sentence
  • Celebrate progress, not just perfect scores

Sentence Practice

  • Use the "Read Aloud" button to hear sentences
  • Ask your child to read sentences with highlighted words
  • Point out sight words in books you read together

Quick Tip for Parents

Focus on one level at a time. Start with Foundation level and move to higher levels as your child's confidence grows. Just 5-10 minutes of daily practice can make a big difference!
